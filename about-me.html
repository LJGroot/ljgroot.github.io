My Background

After obtaining my Bachelor's degree from the College of Child Development and Education (UvA), and a Research Master's degree from the Graduate School of Child Development and Education (UvA), I pursued a doctoral degree at the Research Institute of Child Development and Education (UvA), as a member of the Methods and Statistics research group. Currently, I am a postdoctoral researcher at the Amsterdam School for Communication Research (UvA), where I study online media consumption as a part of the Macroscope project.
