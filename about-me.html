About me

Hi, I'm Lennert.

Although I'm not a mathematician, I am really into numbers. As a toddler, I was already preoccupied with data, rigurously counting and calculating how many motorcycles I saw on the road, even before I had ever been to school. Not much has changed, given the Garmin ever around my wrist and the elaborate spreadheets I maintain to track my progression in rock climbing.

Having obtained a Bachelors degree from the College of Child Development and Education, and a subsequent Reseach Masters degree from the Graduate School of Child Development and Education, I'm currently pursuing a doctoral degree at the Research Institute of Child Development and Education, as a member of the Methods and Statistics research group.
